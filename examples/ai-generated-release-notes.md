# AI-Generated Release Notes

## Problem Statement
Release notes are inconsistent, manual, and often delayed, leading to miscommunication and inefficiencies within product teams.

## Goals
- Streamline the release notes process
- Ensure consistency and timeliness of release notes
- Reduce manual workload for product and engineering managers

## Non-Goals
- Create release notes without any input from users
- Replace all manual processes related to product management

## Target Users
- Product managers
- Engineering managers

## User Stories
### User Story 1
As a product manager, I want to generate release notes from meeting notes and Jira summaries so that I can save time and ensure consistency.

Acceptance Criteria:
  - Release notes are generated from structured meeting notes and Jira summaries
  - Generated notes require minimal manual editing, with examples of acceptable formats provided
  - Users can review and approve the generated notes before distribution, with a clear approval process outlined

## Risks
- AI may misinterpret data leading to inaccurate release notes
- Users may not have structured meeting notes or Jira summaries available
- Potential user resistance to adopting the new process
- Training may be required for users to effectively utilize the tool

## Dependencies
- Access to structured meeting notes
- Access to Jira summaries
- [object Object]

## Success Metrics
- Reduction in time spent creating release notes by 50%
- Increase in user satisfaction with the release notes process by 30%
- Decrease in the number of revisions required post-generation by 40%

## Open Questions
- What level of detail is required in the release notes?
- How will users provide feedback on the generated notes?
- What specific formats or templates do users prefer for release notes?
